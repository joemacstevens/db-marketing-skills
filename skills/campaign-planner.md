# Skill: Campaign Planner

## Purpose
Plan multi-day/week marketing campaigns, seasonal promotions, challenges, and content calendars for Different Breed.

## Before You Start
Read ALL brand context files — campaigns touch everything.

## Campaign Types

### Seasonal Promotions
- **New Year / Resolution Season** (Jan): "Your resolution ends here — at DB" energy
- **Spring Push** (Mar-Apr): Outdoor training, new member drive
- **Summer Body** (May-Jun): Intensity ramp-up, challenge series
- **Back to School** (Aug-Sep): Kids Boxing enrollment push
- **Holiday Hustle** (Nov-Dec): "Don't let the holidays break your discipline"

### Challenges
- 30-day challenges (attendance, fitness goals)
- Weekly themes (Boxing Week, Pilates Power Week)
- Coach challenges ("CJ's Core Challenge")
- Community challenges (bring a friend, member referrals)

### Events
- Open houses / free day events
- Competitions / sparring nights
- Community events (Walk to Wellness, etc.)
- Holiday schedule changes

### Content Series
- "Coach Corner" — weekly coach spotlight rotation
- "Why I Train at DB" — member stories
- "DB History" — origin story, milestones
- "Technique Tuesday" — boxing/fitness tips

## Planning Principles
- **Consistency > virality** — show up every day, don't chase trends
- **Mix content types** — never 3 motivational posts in a row
- **Build momentum** — campaigns should escalate in energy
- **End with CTA** — every campaign drives toward an action (join, book, attend)
- **Repurpose** — one gym session can yield a Reel, Story, carousel, and quote card

## Quality Checklist
- [ ] Campaign has clear goal and end date?
- [ ] Content types are varied across the calendar?
- [ ] Coaches are featured and tagged appropriately?
- [ ] Both IG + FB versions for every post?
- [ ] Visual assets are specified and achievable?
- [ ] Final CTA is clear and actionable?
