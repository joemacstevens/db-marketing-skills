# Facilities — Different Breed Elite Fitness

DB is **three facilities on one block in Teaneck, NJ** — not one building. **Never** describe it as "under one roof," "one room," or a single space. It's a block, three distinct spaces.

1. **Boxing gym** — the ring, heavy bags, mitt work. Home of The Sweet Science, Women Lace Up Too, Blue Collar Boxing, and youth boxing.
2. **Pilates studio** — Core Control Pilates by Menukhah (reformer + mat).
3. **Pulse** — the performance center, branded **Pulse**. Home of Pulse Cardio Dance and TRX training.

When copy needs to name the spaces, use: **"a boxing gym, a Pilates studio, and Pulse."**
