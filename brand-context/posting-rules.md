# Posting Rules — Different Breed Elite Fitness

## Platform Requirements

### Instagram
- **Hashtags:** 5 MAX per post (Meta's current limit)
- **Caption:** No hard limit, but keep it punchy. DB voice = short and impactful.
- **Stories:** Used for daily schedule posts (via Upload-Post automation)
- **Reels:** For high-energy video content, coach spotlights, gym highlights
- **Carousels:** Editorial template style (see visual style guide)
- **Caption editing:** Available via Graph API with `comment_enabled=true`

### Facebook
- **Caption limit:** 255 characters MAX
- **EVERY Instagram post also goes to Facebook** — no exceptions
- **Adapt captions:** FB version must be shorter/punchier than IG version

## Publishing Pipeline
- **Upload-Post** is the preferred publishing tool for IG + FB
- Account name: `dbelitefitness` (CASE-SENSITIVE)
- Param is `title` not `caption`, `user` not `profile`
- Large uploads go background — poll with `getStatus(requestId)`
- Schedule posts go as **STORIES** (not Reels)

## Tagging & Credits
- **@veteranwithacamera** — MUST be credited on ANY post using his footage
- Tag coaches when their classes/content are featured
- Tag coaches in photo AND mention in caption on Instagram

## Content Calendar Defaults
- Post 3-5x per week minimum
- Mix content types: motivational, class promo, coach spotlight, behind-the-scenes
- Daily schedule stories (automated)
- Avoid posting same content type back-to-back

- Coach energy, not marketing energy
- Authenticity > polish
