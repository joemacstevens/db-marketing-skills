# Voice & Tone — Different Breed Elite Fitness

## The Founder
**Don Stevens** — Boxing background, Golden Gloves champion, built DB from the ground up. His philosophy drives everything.

## Core Values
- **Discipline. Desire. Dedication.** — This is the standard.
- "That is the standard we hold ourselves to — and the standard we'll coach you to every single day."

## Taglines
- "Evolve into greatness"
- "What we do is absolutely different"
- "Strength. Individuality. Elite performance."

## Voice Character
- **Gritty** — Real gym, real work. Not a boutique fitness brand.
- **Motivational** — Push people to be better, but from a place of genuine care
- **Community-first** — DB is a family, not just a gym
- **Direct** — Don's voice: "Standards over hype." "No fluff, just work."
- **Coach energy** — A coach talking to athletes, never a brand talking to consumers

## What We Sound Like
✅ "Walk through the door. Be different."
✅ "No gimmicks. Just work."
✅ "This isn't a gym. It's a standard."
✅ "Every rep. Every round. Earn it."

## What We DON'T Sound Like
❌ "Join our fitness community today! 🎉"
❌ "Ready to transform your body? Sign up now!"
❌ "We offer a wide range of fitness classes..."
❌ Corporate, salesy, generic fitness marketing

## Tone Adjustments by Content Type
- **Motivational posts:** Raw, punchy, short sentences. Like a coach's halftime speech.
- **Class promos:** Informational but still energetic. Include time/coach/what to expect.
- **Coach spotlights:** Warm, respectful, highlight their expertise and personality.
- **Event announcements:** Urgent energy. "Don't miss this."
- **Behind the scenes:** Casual, authentic. Let the gym speak for itself.
- **Kids Boxing:** Supportive, community pride. Highlight discipline and growth for young athletes.
- **Pilates:** Strength-focused, not "gentle stretching." DB Pilates is intense.
