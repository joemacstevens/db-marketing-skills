# Target Audiences — Different Breed Elite Fitness

## Primary: Serious Fitness Enthusiasts (25-45)
- People who want more than a Planet Fitness experience
- Looking for real coaching, not just equipment access
- Value discipline, structure, and community
- Willing to push themselves and be pushed

## Secondary: Parents of Young Athletes
- Kids Boxing parents — want discipline, confidence, fitness for their children
- Value the structured, coach-led environment
- Community-minded — often become members themselves
- Age range of kids: typically 6-14

## Tertiary: Pilates / Strength Seekers
- Women (and men) looking for serious Pilates — not gentle stretching
- Interested in Core Control Pilates specifically
- May cross over into boxing/general fitness
- Value early morning and weekend class options

## Geographic
- Local New Jersey community
- People within driving distance of the gym
- Strong word-of-mouth / referral culture

## What Unites All Audiences
- They want to be **challenged**, not coddled
- They're looking for **community**, not just a workout
- They respect **expertise** and real credentials
- They're drawn to **authenticity** over polish
- They want to feel like they're part of something **different**

## Messaging by Audience
- **Serious athletes:** Lead with intensity, credentials, facility quality
- **Parents:** Lead with discipline, character development, safe environment, coach quality
- **Pilates seekers:** Lead with instructor expertise, class variety, "not your average Pilates"
- **General fitness:** Lead with community, transformation stories, the DB difference
