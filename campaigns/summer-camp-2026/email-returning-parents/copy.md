## Copy Notes

- **Opening "Hey {kidName}'s parent"** — intentionally informal. Don knows these people.
- **"You're getting this before anyone else"** — priority access is the key hook. Don't bury it.
- **Lunch rule spelled out explicitly** — M-Th pack lunch, Friday pizza — protects against parent confusion/complaints.
- **Price table anchor** — early bird urgency at the bottom, not the top. Don leads with relationship, closes with urgency.
- **"No gimmicks. Just work."** — Don's phrase, available but kept out of this version to avoid redundancy with "We don't babysit. We build." Two counter-narrative phrases in one email is one too many.
- **CTA text:** "LOCK IN {kidName}'S SPOT →" — personalized button text outperforms generic "Register Now" on warm lists.
