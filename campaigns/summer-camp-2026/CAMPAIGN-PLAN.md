## KEY MESSAGES (Rotate Through All Content)

1. **"This isn't summer babysitting. This is athlete development."**
   — DB Camp is elite training, not generic daycare with sports. Kids come out different.

2. **"5 coaches. Real attention. No one gets lost."**
   — Low camper-to-coach ratio = real coaching, real accountability, real growth.

3. **"Sports performance. Boxing. Basketball. Conditioning. Yoga. Nine weeks of everything."**
   — The variety angle. No other camp in the area offers this range at this level.

4. **"Discipline doesn't take the summer off."**
   — Speaks to parents. Keep the standard going year-round.

5. **"Your kid will come home tired. They'll be back the next day."**
   — Authentic proof point. The program is hard enough and fun enough that kids want to return.

6. **"Early bird pricing ends June 12. After that, prices go up."**
   — Urgency driver for the pricing push.

7. **"We've been doing this. The photos don't lie."**
   — Leverages 2025 camp archive. Real proof over marketing promises.
