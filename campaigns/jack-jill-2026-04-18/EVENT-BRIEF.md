# Jack & Jill × Different Breed — Wellness Event

**Date:** April 18, 2026
**Partner:** Jack and Jill of America, Inc. — Bergen-Passaic Chapter
**DB Host:** Don Sommerville + DB coaches
**Jack & Jill Liaison:** Coach Tara (Health & Wellness Committee chair)

## What It Was
A multi-station family wellness Saturday at Different Breed. Jack & Jill is a mothers' organization for Black families — charitable + educational. Goal: raise our kids to be proud of themselves and their community. DB hosted the gym for the day so mothers, fathers, and children could experience DB together.

## Stations (confirmed from footage)
- **Main floor (green turf):** Kids agility + sprint drills — Coach Joe + Coach Ali
- **Boxing ring:** Kids boxing combos with coach (@joebutta25 in red DB tank)
- **Pulse studio:** Adult TRX class (pushups, hip raises) + Zumba dance break
- **Mixed family:** Pogo / bounding drills — kids + parents together
- **"Dedication!" group photo:** Everyone under the Dedication sign, group cheer
