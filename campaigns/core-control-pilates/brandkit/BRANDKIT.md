# Pilates by Menukhah — Brand Kit (Operational v1)

This is the working production brand kit for flyers and social assets.

## 1) Brand intent
Women-only, boutique, calm confidence, elevated but approachable.

   - include package pricing: 5 classes for $100, 10 classes for $180
2. **Chinese auction flyer**

## 3) Colors
Use `brandkit/colors.json` as source-of-truth.

## 4) Typography
Use `brandkit/type-system.md`.

Preferred:
- Headline: Cormorant Garamond
- Subhead/UI: Montserrat
- Body: Lora

## 5) Layout style
- Keep breathing room and generous margins.
- Use one strong studio image + one text panel.
- Avoid clutter and over-layered badges.
- Keep CTA and pricing blocks obvious at first glance.

## 6) Visual motifs
- Rounded cards / soft corners
- Neutral overlays (cream/ivory)
- Thin separators and subtle texture
- High legibility over artistic effects
