## Recommended stack (preferred)
### Display / Headline
- **Cormorant Garamond** (Google Font)
  - Elegant serif, boutique wellness feel.

### Subhead / UI labels
- **Montserrat** (Google Font)
  - Clean, modern, readable in all-caps and small labels.

### Body copy
- **Lora** (Google Font)
  - Friendly serif body text for short-form marketing copy.

## Alternate stack (if you want more luxury editorial)
- Display: **Playfair Display** (Google)
- Subhead: **Manrope** (Google)
- Body: **Source Sans 3** (Google)

## Adobe alternatives
- Display: **Kepler Std** or **Adobe Garamond Pro**
- Sans: **Acumin Pro** or **Proxima Nova**

## Sizing guidance (flyer)
- H1: 56–78 px (tracking +1 to +3)
- H2/Subhead: 30–42 px
- Body: 18–24 px
- Label/chips: 14–18 px (semi-bold)
- Fine print: 12–14 px

## Hierarchy rules
- Max 2 font families per flyer.
- Keep headline to 2 lines max when possible.
- Use sans-serif for numeric pricing for clarity.
