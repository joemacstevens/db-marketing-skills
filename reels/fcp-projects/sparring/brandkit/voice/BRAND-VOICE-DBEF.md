# Different Breed Elite Fitness — Brand Voice (Seed v1)

## Source Inputs
1. Website copy and current class/program positioning
2. Owner podcast transcript (`brandkit/research/owner-podcast-raw-transcript.md`)

## Voice Intent
Speak like a coach who cares and expects effort. Practical, direct, encouraging, never fluffy.

## Tone Traits
- **Direct:** say what matters in plain language
- **Accountable:** consistency over motivation spikes
- **Inclusive but disciplined:** all levels welcome, standards stay high
- **Local and real:** community-first, no fake hype
- **Action-focused:** every message should drive a next step

## Messaging Pillars
1. **Earned progress** — results come from consistency and form
2. **Coaching + community** — people stay because they’re seen and pushed
3. **Performance mindset** — show up, execute, improve
4. **Sustainable fitness** — real life, long-term habits, no gimmicks

## Copy Rules (for site + content)
- Keep sentences short and punchy
- Use verbs: train, build, commit, improve, show up
- Avoid corporate wellness clichés
- Avoid over-promising transformations
- Always include a concrete CTA

## Approved Vocabulary
- Different Breed
- Show up
- Put in the work
- Consistency
- Build strength
- Accountability
- Real results

## Avoid
- "Life-changing in 7 days"
- "Secret hack" language
- Generic influencer tone
- Overly technical jargon for beginners

## CTA Bank
- Book your first class
- See this week’s schedule
- Start with a fundamentals session
- Train with us this week
- DM us to get started

## Social/Post Formula
1. Hook (1 line)
2. Truth (2–3 lines, direct)
3. Action (1 clear CTA)
