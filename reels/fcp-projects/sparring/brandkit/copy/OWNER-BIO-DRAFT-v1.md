## Medium Bio (About Page)
Different Breed Elite Fitness was built for people who want more than motivation. Under Don Somerville's leadership, the gym emphasizes disciplined training, high standards, and practical coaching that meets people where they are.

The mission is straightforward: help members show up, put in the work, and get stronger over time. Programs are designed to balance challenge and support so beginners and experienced athletes can both progress in a real, no-hype environment.

At Different Breed, fitness is not a short sprint. It is a long-term commitment powered by consistency, coaching, and community.

## Long Bio (Press/Partner)
Don Somerville, owner of Different Breed Elite Fitness has built the brand around one core belief: real progress is earned, not promised. Through a coaching-first approach, Different Breed combines structure, accountability, and community to help members build sustainable strength, confidence, and performance.

From class programming to culture, the focus remains on showing up, executing well, and improving week by week. That philosophy has shaped Different Breed into more than a gym. It is a training community for people who want honest coaching and long-term results.
